\begin{mdef}[Residues]
This is how we define the stuff
A residue is the $c_{-1}$ term in the Laurent expansion of $f$:
\begin{equation}
f(z) = \sum_{-\infty}^\infty
	\left[ \frac{1}{2\pi i}
		\oint_\gamma \frac{f(z)}{(z-c)^{n+1}}dz
	\right]
	\left(z-c\right)^n
\end{equation}
\end{mdef}
